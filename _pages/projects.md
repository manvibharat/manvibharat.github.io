Below are selected research projects aligned with my current interests in  
control, reinforcement learning, trajectory optimization, and multi-agent systems.

---

### Geometric Control on SO(3) with Hardware-in-the-Loop Validation  
**MS Thesis, IIT Madras**

Implemented geometric attitude control laws on the Lie group SO(3) using Julia  
and DifferentialEquations.jl. Developed a hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) setup for  
evaluating real-time behavior, stability, and noise robustness of quadrotor-style  
rotational dynamics.

---

### Curvature-Adaptive Trajectory Optimization for Quadrotors  
Designed curvature-aware and spline-based trajectory generation strategies for  
smooth 3D paths. Developed waypoint pruning, smoothing, and online replanning  
methods to reduce oscillations and improve tracking accuracy.
